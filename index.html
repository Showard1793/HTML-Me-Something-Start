The Legend of Kaia

In the faraway land of home-to-home, Kaia awaited her next great adventure. As a stray in the mean streets of St. Louis, her life was never easy. To say it bluntly, she scrounged by - living meal to meal and dealing with a colorful arrray of cads and scoundrels to make ends meet. Yet, through it all, her spirit remained unbroken, her heart full of hope for a better tomorrow.
